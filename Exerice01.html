About Indian Cricket

Indian cricket has produced some of the greatest batsmen, bowlers, and all-rounders the world has ever seen. India has won the ICC Cricket World Cup in 1983 and 2011, the ICC T20 World Cup in 2007, and numerous other tournaments.

🌟 Cricket Legends 🌟

Sachin Tendulkar

The Master Blaster

Born: 24 April 1973 | Mumbai

Sachin is the highest run-scorer in both Test and ODI cricket history. He scored 100 international centuries - a feat no one has matched.

Tests: 200 | Runs: 15,921 | Avg: 53.78

MS Dhoni

Captain Cool

Born: 7 July 1981 | Ranchi

Widely regarded as India's greatest captain. Won the 2007 T20 World Cup, 2011 Cricket World Cup, and 2013 Champions Trophy.

ODIs: 350 | Runs: 10,773 | Stumpings: 123

Virat Kohli

Run Machine

Born: 5 November 1988 | Delhi

One of the finest batsmen of the modern era. Holds the record for most ODI centuries while chasing.

Tests: 113+ | Runs: 8,000+ | ODI Centuries: 46+

Rohit Sharma

Hitman

Born: 30 April 1987 | Nagpur

The only batsman to score three double centuries in ODIs. India's Test and ODI captain.

ODIs: 260+ | Runs: 10,000+ | Highest: 264

Jasprit Bumrah

The Yorker King

Born: 6 December 1993 | Ahmedabad

One of the best fast bowlers in the world. His unorthodox action and lethal yorkers make him virtually unplayable.

Tests: 40+ | Wickets: 180+ | Best: 6/27

💡 Did You Know?

🏏 Fact 1: India played their first Test match in 1932 against England.

🏏 Fact 2: The iconic "Howzat" shout originates from the Hindi "Hai zaat" meaning "Is it out?"

🏏 Fact 3: Sachin Tendulkar received the Bharat Ratna, India's highest civilian honour, in 2014.

🏏 Fact 4: The IPL (Indian Premier League) is the world's richest T20 cricket league.

🏏 Fact 5: India has never lost a home Test series since 2012.
