World History Timeline

A brief overview of significant eras.

Ancient Era (3000 BCE - 500 CE)

The rise of early civilizations in Mesopotamia, Egypt, India, and China. Development of writing and early empires.

Middle Ages (500 CE - 1500 CE)

Feudalism in Europe, the Golden Age of Islam, and the Mongol Empire's expansion.

Early Modern Period (1500 - 1800)

The Renaissance, Age of Discovery, Scientific Revolution, and the Enlightenment.

Modern Era (1800 - Present)

Industrial Revolution, World Wars, the Space Age, and the Digital Revolution.
