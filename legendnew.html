About Malayalam Cinema

Malayalam cinema, known as "Mollywood", is a prominent Indian film industry based in Kerala, celebrated for its realistic storytelling, high technical quality, and strong literary roots. Originating in the 1920s, it gained national acclaim in the 1950s for social realism.
